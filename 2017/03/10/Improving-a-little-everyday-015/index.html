From now on we will be calling these “features” that we import modules. I’ll say things like, “You want to import the sys module.” They are also called “libraries” by other programmers, but let’s just stick with modules.
从今往后，我们将称呼“features”为导入模块。我将说”你要导入sys模块”。它们也被其他程序员称为库，但我们还是叫它模块。

Many times searching for “python THING” will find answers to what that THING does in Python. Try searching for “python open.”
很多时候搜索“python ** ”可以找到python里对应的东西。试着搜索“python open”。

but commands are also called “functions” and “methods.”
